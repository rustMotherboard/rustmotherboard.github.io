mindvisor shimeji

have you ever wanted to fling around the characters from hit game mindvisor across your desktop for some unknown reason? wanna look at them kinda just go around your desktop and sometimes crawl on the floor?

well for one, what kind of person even are you?

for two, now you can! the official (super official) mindvisor shimeji are now available for public download!

the mindvisor shimeji download contains the following characters:

  • rikotsura (riko)
  • kado
  • ajax
  • javion (jav)

also included are the older variants for ajax and jav (kado and riko only have one variant)

preview


old versions also included

installation instructions

you'll need shimeji-ee in order to use these (requires java and windows)

inside the contents of the download are a handful of folders, take those and drag them into [install directory]\shimejiee\img. once that's done, run the shimeji-ee .jar file, right click on its icon in the system tray, go to settings -> choose shimeji, and then select the ones you wanna use

scratchxt collection

this is one of the larger parts of the downloads page. what you're seeing here is over 2 GB worth of 600+ projects that were made during my time with the programming site named scratch, a visual-based programming language designed for youngins

with a clunky graphic editor and some horrible spelling, the scratchxt collection contains some of my first work in the digital space, including some bonus projects!

this collection contains three categories, the main projects from the accounts ScratchXT, ScratchXTRises, and ravenGenetrix. the latestremixes folder has the latest remixes of projects (such as ones that encourage adding your OC, have that be remixed, then remix that, etc), and the bonus folder contains misc projects that i thought were funny or some highlights of certain remixes. gifts also fall under this

(note that you'll need turbowarp desktop in other to load these projects, a fork of scratch that runs a lot faster than vanilla scratch)

it'll take a while to download, so You May Want to Get Some Snacks.
