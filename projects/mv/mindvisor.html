mindvisor

mindvisor is a yume nikki fan-game about a girl named rikotsura, who receives a prototype for a headset capable of granting users the ability to explore their dreams while conscious, and manipulate them through thoughts alone.

although exploring your subconscious and reliving memories might seem like a good idea at first, perhaps there are some things which are best left forgotten... or at least things you'd rather not think about.

the game's still being worked on...no plans to announce a release date
