RWWSNC

RWWSNC (Reasons Why we Should Never Chart) is a keyboard focused stepmania/etterna pack made by me and charlotte (although as of late it's been mostly my charts). i'm sure you already know what stepmania and etterna are, 4 button arrow rhythm game.

song list

as of right now the main pack is split into 3 smaller packs

  • RWWSNC (the main pack)
  • RWWSNC0.5 (charts that sucked so i took them out of the main pack)
  • RWWSNC0.6 (dave and bambi related tracks. there were too many)

as of 10/28/2023 there's 20 + 8 + 8 = 36 total tracks across all three packs. i'm thinking 50 total before release, but if i don't have any good ideas for new songs, i'll just polish it for release.

release

i'm still working on the pack from time to time, and i'll release it when i think it's all ready for release. the release should ALSO work on quaver, but i haven't tested it
