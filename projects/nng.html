nichijou no gaiden

Nichijou No Gaiden (abbreviated as NNG) was a web-comic/animation series that i made a while ago. it was one of my first big projects. NNG was going to be a mix of the characters from Nichijou with the style of Battle for BFDI, an animated series by jacknjellify, which that kinda styled was used in CFAN4/6 instead.

NNG was canceled due to a lack of proper structure, poor planning, mistreatment, and having too much planned at once. it was going to be an animation, then a web-comic, then both, a visual novel was considered, there's a subreddit and Wikia page that have been untouched, and two now defunct brand accounts made, being Team Gaiden and Taugamma.

all that currently remains now are the promotional material, scripts, and assets. Some web-comic pages were done, but nothing had come out of them.
