Nichijou No Gaiden (abbreviated as NNG) was a web-comic/animation series that I made a while ago. It was one of my first big projects, although now that title belongs to klaust_. NNG was going to be a mix of the characters from Nichijou with the style of Battle for BFDI, a series by Jacknjellify.

NNG was canceled for several reasons, although most were my fault due to a lack of proper structure, poor planning, mistreatment, and having too much planned at once. It was going to be an animation, then a web-comic, then both, a visual novel was considered, there's a subreddit and Wikia page that have been untouched, and two brand accounts made, being Team Gaiden and Taugamma. Both groups are gone now, for your information.

All that currently remains now are the promotional material, scripts, and assets. Some web-comic pages were done, but nothing had come out of them.

I have tried to bring this project back a few times, however I've been unable to allocate any time for it, and I feel that it's still too much of a mess for me to come back to. Assets and scripts are available at the downloads page.
